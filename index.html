A varied practice, tailored to your level

Vinyasa

Dynamic, breath-led flow linking movement and breath. Builds strength, stamina, and focus. Expect creative sequencing with options at every level — challenge yourself or find a softer edge.

Mandala

A 360-degree elemental practice that moves around the mat, targeting different muscle groups and energy lines. Playful, rhythmic, and deeply introspective — a style you won’t find in many studios.

Yin

Long-held, floor-based postures that work deep into the connective tissue and joints. Stillness that challenges the mind as much as the body. The perfect counterbalance to a dynamic morning practice.

Yoga Nidra

Guided “yogic sleep” — a deeply restorative meditation practised lying down. One session is said to equal several hours of rest. The way you’ll want to end every day.

Questions

Good to know

What level of yoga experience do I need?

This retreat is open to all levels. Whether you're a seasoned practitioner or relatively new to yoga, sessions will be adapted to support you wherever you are in your practice.

How do I get there?

The Yoga Loft Retreat is located in the Dordogne region of Aquitaine, France — just an hour from two regional airports. We’ll share detailed travel information and transfer options once your place is confirmed.

What should I bring?

Comfortable clothes for practice, swimwear, and anything you need for a relaxing few days. Mats and props are provided. A full packing guide will be sent before the retreat.

Can I come alone?

Absolutely. Many retreaters come solo — it’s a wonderful way to meet like-minded people. The shared room option is perfect if you’re happy to pair up, or choose an individual room for your own space.

What is the cancellation policy?

We understand that plans can change. Full details of the cancellation policy are available here: https://www.activexplore.co.uk/terms-and-conditions

Are dietary requirements catered for?

Yes. Our private chef is experienced in accommodating a range of dietary needs. Please let us know at the time of booking and we’ll make sure you’re well looked after.

Do I need travel insurance?

Yes — all guests must have their own travel insurance in place before the retreat. This should cover trip cancellation, medical expenses, and repatriation. We’ll send a reminder when you book.

Do I need to be a certain fitness level?

The retreat is designed for all levels, but yoga is a physical practice. When you book, we’ll send a short health questionnaire — similar to what you’d complete at a gym — so we can adapt sessions to any needs. If you have specific injuries or conditions, just let us know and we’ll work with you.

How is my trip protected?

We have partnered with ActiveXplore Travel to fully financially protect your trip. As a result all payments will be handled by ActiveXplore Travel via our retreat's designated regulated trust reference.
